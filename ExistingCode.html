Intel® Silicon Photonics

Intel® Silicon Photonics Optical Transceivers from TD SYNNEX are re-defining performance and scalability for data traffic. Bringing together the power of optics and the scalability of silicon, Intel® Silicon Photonics are a range of pluggable optical transceivers which can be incorporated into a wide range of Ethernet switches, routers and networking equipment.

Key Features

  • Delivers optical connectivity on a single piece of silicon - reliable, scalable and cost effective
  • Enables high bandwidth, software-configurable access to compute and storage devices
  • Provides optical connectivity to other technologies:- Ethernet switches, Smart-NICs, and FPGAs
  • Leverages the integrity of the Industry's leading optical transceiver brand - Intel®
